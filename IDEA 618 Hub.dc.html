The IDEA Section 618 Data Collections Hub

Every year, each state reports how many infants, toddlers, children, and students with disabilities it serves under IDEA Section 618. This site collects those reports in one place: read the national trends, look up any state, and compare over time. All figures are reported counts, not estimates.
