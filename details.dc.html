A glass house full of light

Dedicated in 1936, its fifty-foot glass walls step back in tiers so hail bounces right off. Inside: lily pools, tropical green, light everywhere. The plants are the décor, exactly as Juli wanted.

Garden semi-formal

Think warm terracotta, blush, sage, and ivory. Dress up, feel comfortable, and pick shoes happy on stone garden paths. It's a greenhouse, so expect a little cozy warmth.

Parking & arrival

Free parking sits just beside The Jewel Box in Forest Park. Please arrive about 20 minutes early to settle in among the ferns. Both venues are wheelchair accessible, with ramped entrances, accessible restrooms, and accessible parking close by.

The relaxed part

No wedding party, no fuss, just the people we love in a room full of plants. Come ready to celebrate, and don't forget to play along with our little guest game.
