The Setting

A glass house full of light

Built in 1936, The Jewel Box is a soaring Art Deco greenhouse: walls of stepped glass, tropical greenery, and lily pools. The plants are the décor, exactly as Juli wanted.

What to Wear

Garden semi-formal

Think warm terracotta, blush, sage, and ivory. Dress up, feel comfortable, and pick shoes happy on stone garden paths. It's a greenhouse, so expect a little cozy warmth.

Getting There

Parking & arrival

Free parking sits just beside The Jewel Box off Wells Drive in Forest Park. Please arrive about 20 minutes early to find a seat and settle in among the ferns.
